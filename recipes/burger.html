Burger Recipe

Description

This burger recipe will blow your mind!

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Sliced Tomatos
  • Chopped lettuce
  • One egg
  • 200 grams ground beef
  • Spices of choice
  • Ketchup/Mayo
  1. Move the beef to a bowl and add the spices, mix together and add an egg
  2. Heat a pan to medium heat and add t tbls of olive oil
  3. Add the beef to the pan and cook until brown
  4. Get the buns on a plate and start putting on the topings
  5. put the burger on the buns
  6. Enjoy!
