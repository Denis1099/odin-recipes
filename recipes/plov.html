Plov Recipe

Description

This plov recipe will blow your mind!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs Beef chuck, beef sirloin or good quality beef stew meat
  • 1/3 cup canola oil, or extra light olive oil
  • 2 medium onions
  • 3 medium carrots, cut into matchsticks or grated
  • 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 3-4 bay leaves
  • 1 head of garlic
  1. pat the meat dry with a paper towel and chop into 1/2″ to 3/4″ pieces.
  2. Preheat your dutch oven
  3. Reduce heat to medium and Add chopped onion
  4. dd 1 3/4 cups hot water, cover & simmer over medium/low heat 45 min or until meat is tender.
  5. Meanwhile, rinse rice until water runs clear, then drain and set aside (this gets rid of the starch so you wont end up with a sticky rice)
  6. Enjoy!
